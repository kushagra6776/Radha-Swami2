BRIDGING DREAMS

Blurring the line between the rich and poor, at Radhaswami Orgainsation, we envision a future where every child has the chance to embrace quality education, every family can find solace in a secure home, and every individual can rise above financial hardships. We're crafting a society where every hope triumphs over despair, and opportunities are the stepping stones to a brighter future.

OUR STORY

Established in the year 2010 in Patna, the Radhaswami Organisation was founded by Mr. Deepak Sharma. The organisation aims to create a better society, providing a platform that would not only provide essential support but an environment for dreams to flourish.
Since its inception, the organisation is working towards uplifting the education standards of the backward class people. In 2017 the organisation started a school in Patna which is now in the list of top 10 schools in Patna. Till now it has opened 4 schools and aims to open at least one school in each district with quality education.
Radhaswami Organisation provides financial support through various schemes such as Aawas Yojana, Vahan Yojana, Kanyadaan Yojana, Loan Maaf Yojana, Shiksha Yojana, and many more. To date, many people from various communities have benefitted from these schemes.
Radhaswami Organisation extends its hand of warmth and support to communities in multiple states across India, including Bihar, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, West Bengal, Assam, Jammu and Kashmir, Uttarakhand, Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Gujarat, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Maharashtra. The organisation recognizes that needs knows no boundaries, and the diverse presence showcases the promise to never leave anyone behind.

OUR SCHEMES

KANYADAAN
YOJANA

At Radhaswami Orgainsation we believe in
the joyous celebration of every occasion
without any burden. The Kanyadaan Yojana
is one such scheme that relieves your
financial burden while celebrating your
daughter's wedding. Although marriage
brings two families and souls together, it
incurs a cost of a heavy financial burden.
Through this yojana, we are here to share
this financial burden with you.

  • Available for girls ages 0-10 years, this
    yojana is a testament to our dedication
    to work for the upliftment of our society.
  • The beneficiary of the yojana will get an
    amount of up to 1 lakh for the daughter's
    marriage expenses.
  • The amount is complemented by gifts -
    from essential household items like a
    bed, sofa set, and dressing table to
    electrical appliances like a television
    set, washing machine, mixer grinder,
    and jewelry as well.

Kanyadaan Yojana, more than a yojana is a
celebration of love, empowerment, and the
promise of a bright future ahead.

VAHAAN
YOJANA

The Vahan Yojana by Radhaswami
Organisation empowers you with the
freedom of mobility. Our aim is to
empower people by freeing them from the
financial burden of mobility. This Yojana is
a testament to our commitment to
improving lives by providing greater
mobility and independence.

  • Under this yojana, you get access to a
    vehicle (Vahan) of your choice at a
    discount of up to 40%.
  • Whether it's a two-wheeler or a four-
    wheeler, the yojana opens the door for
    an opportunity to travel with ease and
    efficiency.
  • To empower people and make them
    free from financial burdens, Vahan
    Yojana ensures to make mobility more
    affordable.

AAWAS
YOJANA

Radhaswami Organisation's Aawas Yojana
mirrors the national mission, with a
localized touch. We're committed to
providing sturdy, sustainable homes to
families in need. Our efforts go beyond
construction; we're building communities
and nurturing hope for a brighter, secure
future.

  • Aawas Yojana is dedicated to
    providing the gift of a house at just ₹
    1,31,000.
  • You can build a 2 BHK house on your
    land or opt for a 2 BHK flat in the
    village.
  • With a period of 3 years for completion
    of construction work, the whole
    process is properly planned with a
    focus on quality and durability.
  • Top-notch materials are being used
    from renowned companies in the
    completion of the construction job.

At Radhaswami Organisation, we
understand the significance of a home and
through the Aawas Yojana, we aim to build
not just a house, but a place of comfort
and security.

VAHAAN
YOJANA

The Loan Maafi Yojana extends a helping
hand to you to remove the burden of
loans, whether personal or home loans. It's
an effort to get rid of financial burdens and
provide a fresh start to your financial goals.

  • Under this yojana, Radhaswami
    Organisation is committed to assisting
    you by covering 40% of the loan amount.
  • This yojana will allow you to get rid of
    the weight of financial problems,
    paving the way for a debt-free future.
  • Radhaswami Organisation through this
    initiative aims to let you regain control
    of your own financial destinies.

OUR PRESIDENT

DEEPAK SHARMA
NATIONAL PRESIDENT

Radhaswami Sangathan

To uplift the underserved families, I believe in
providing education as the key to unlocking their
journey towards self-empowerment and achieving
lasting financial stability."

"

Born in 1988, in a village named Kandi in Jharkhand, Deepak
Sharma is a dedicated social worker and the force behind the
Radhaswami Organisation. Being a teacher himself in his
formative years, he knows the importance of education and
through the Radhaswami Organisation is empowering the
students from poor families with proper education.

With a sense of empathy and a profound understanding of the issues faced
by poor families, he has channeled his attention towards the mission to
break the poverty cycle and impart financial independence to the financially
weaker population. Through his efforts, he's not only transforming lives but
also creating a society free from financial burdens.
